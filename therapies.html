Common Questions

Everything You Need To Know

Is IV therapy safe?

Yes. Every session is overseen by a licensed medical provider and formulated using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients. A health intake is required before your first visit.

How long does a session take?

Most infusions take between 45 and 60 minutes. You are welcome to relax, read, or work during your session.

How often should I come in?

This depends on your goals. Many clients visit once or twice a month for maintenance, while others come weekly for performance support.

Will I feel results right away?

Most clients feel a noticeable difference during or immediately after their session. Effects typically last two to four days and build over time with consistent visits.

Do I need a prescription?

No prescription is needed. Our medical staff reviews your intake and health history to ensure the right formula is selected for you.

Can I drive after my session?

Absolutely. IV therapy does not impair your ability to drive. You can return to your normal daily activities right after your session.
