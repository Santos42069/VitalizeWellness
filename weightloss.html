Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why 3 sessions specifically?

Fat metabolism through IV infusion works best in a cumulative protocol. The first session primes your metabolism, the second delivers the peak fat-targeting effect, and the third reinforces and locks in results. Trying to compress it into one session would significantly reduce effectiveness.

How far apart should the sessions be?

We recommend spacing sessions 1 to 2 weeks apart. This gives your body enough time to process each infusion fully while maintaining the metabolic momentum needed for compounding results.

What areas can be targeted?

The systemic portion of the infusion works throughout the body. Common areas where clients notice the most visible changes include the abdomen, flanks, thighs, and arms — areas where stubborn fat tends to persist despite diet and exercise.

Is this the same as Semaglutide or Ozempic?

No. This is a nutrient and compound IV infusion — not a GLP-1 medication. It works through fat oxidation and metabolic support rather than appetite suppression. It can be used alongside GLP-1 medications or as a standalone program.

Will I need to follow a special diet?

There is no strict diet required, but your provider will share hydration and nutrition guidance to help amplify your results between sessions. The more supportive your lifestyle during the program, the more pronounced your results will be.

How long do results last?

Results vary by individual, but many clients maintain their changes for several months, especially when supporting their goals with balanced nutrition and regular movement. Maintenance sessions are available after the initial 3-session program.
