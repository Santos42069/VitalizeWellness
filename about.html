How We Started

Where It All Began

Vitalize was founded in 2019 by Dr. Alberto Cortes, a board-certified family medicine physiciatrist who had grown frustrated with the limitations of traditional clinical care. She saw patients who were technically healthy but felt exhausted, depleted, and disconnected from their own wellbeing.

His answer was a different kind of wellness space — one that combined rigorous medical expertise with a deeply personal, spa-quality experience. A place where you could receive clinical-grade IV infusions while actually enjoying the process.

What started as a single suite has grown into a fully staffed wellness center serving hundreds of clients each month. The mission has never changed: help people feel genuinely, measurably better.
